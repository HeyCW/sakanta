How does Sakanta work?

01
Discover & Co-Own

Explore our handpicked collection of beautiful homes. Pay for the share you need and own a slice of paradise without the full burden of cost.

02
Plan Your Stays, Fairly

Our smart scheduling system ensures that your time in the home reflects your ownership. Own 1/8? Enjoy 1/8 of the year—distributed fairly and flexibly.

03
Hassle-Free Living

Leave the upkeep to us. From regular maintenance to on-call support, our professional concierge team ensures your home stays in top shape—so you can simply arrive and relax.

04
Co-Create Your Home

You’re not just a guest—you’re a co-owner. Sakanta’s transparent voting system gives every co-owner a voice. Want to propose an upgrade or add a personal touch? If no one objects, it’s a go.

05
Resell When You're Ready

No longer using your share or want to adjust your investment? List your share at your chosen price—Sakanta helps connect you with new co-owners and ensures a smooth resale process.
